About Cardiff Pulse

Welcome to Cardiff Pulse, the premier independent news source for the capital of Wales. We are dedicated to bringing you the most timely, accurate, and engaging stories from across the city.


From the bustling streets of Queen Street to the serene waters of Cardiff Bay, our mission is to keep residents and visitors connected to the heart of the city. We cover local politics, culture, events, and community stories that matter most to you.


Cardiff Pulse is powered by community engagement and a passion for storytelling. Whether it's a new restaurant opening in Roath or a major infrastructure update in the city centre, if it's happening in Cardiff, it's on the Pulse.
