About Cardiff Pulse

Cardiff Pulse is the heartbeat of the capital. We are an independent news organization dedicated to bringing you the most relevant stories from across Cardiff.

Founded in 2025, our mission is to provide a platform for local voices and to ensure that the people of Cardiff are kept informed about the issues that matter most to them. We operate with a small, dedicated team based right here in the city.

We believe in journalism that is fast, fair, and bold. No corporate agendas—just the news you need to know.
