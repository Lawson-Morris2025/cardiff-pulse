About Cardiff Pulse

Cardiff Pulse is the heartbeat of the capital. We are an independent news organization dedicated to bringing you the most relevant, hard-hitting, and engaging stories from across Cardiff and the surrounding valleys.

Founded in 2025, our mission is to provide a platform for local voices and to ensure that the people of Cardiff are kept informed about the issues that matter most to them.
